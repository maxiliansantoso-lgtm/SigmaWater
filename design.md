# Sigma Water - Website Design Document

## Aesthetic and Theme
- **Color Palette:** Soft greens, teals, whites, and metallic accents (silver/gray).
- **Typography:** Modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Poppins, or Outfit) for a clean, highly legible look.
- **Vibe:** Clean, refreshing, premium, modern, eco-friendly.
- **Key Elements:** Soft gradients, glassmorphism, 3D product renders (podiums, spheres), leafy natural foreground elements.

## UI Components & Layout (Based on Reference)

### Header
- **Logo:** "Sigma Water" with a stylized water drop icon.
- **Navigation Links:** Home, Our Products, About us, Sales.
- **Call to Action (CTA):** "Pre-order" button with a solid dark background for high contrast.

### Hero Section
- **Left Column:**
  - **Headline:** Highlighting insulation, temperature retention, or premium quality (e.g., "Botol Air Minum Insulasi untuk Suhu Sempurna").
  - **Subheadline:** Exploring benefits of hydration and the risks of dehydration.
  - **Link:** "VIEW PRODUCTS" with an underline or arrow.
  - **Interactive Element:** Bottle color selector (dots showing available colors like teal, green, purple, red).
- **Center Focus:**
  - Large product image (water bottle) resting on a sleek cylindrical podium.
  - Background features soft circular gradients, wireframe lines, and metallic spheres to create a sense of depth and modernity.
- **Right Column:**
  - **Floating Card 1:** "PRE-ORDER" information and a "PRICE LIST" link.
  - **Floating Card 2:** Secondary product image with a "PLAY VIDEO" button overlay to encourage engagement.

## Considerations for the Indonesian Market
- **Mobile Responsiveness:** The layout must be perfectly responsive, as the primary device for internet access in Indonesia is the smartphone. The columns will need to stack vertically (Header -> Center Product -> Left Column Text -> Right Column Cards).
- **Copywriting:** Tone should be persuasive and relatable, potentially mixing formal Bahasa Indonesia with modern conversational terms depending on the target demographic.
- **Loading Speed:** Optimization of the large 3D and high-resolution assets is crucial for users on varying network speeds.
