Our Story

Founded in 2016 and proudly based in the heart of Central Sulawesi, Sigma Water was created with a singular mission: to provide our community with the purest, most refreshing drinking water available.

We combine state-of-the-art filtration technology with a commitment to sustainability, ensuring that every drop you drink—whether from our convenient cups, daily bottles, or household gallons—supports a healthier lifestyle and a healthier planet.
